Read Me:

Concurrency project for a simple traffic controller app

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - 


Displays current time stamps in 1 second intervalsDisplays real-time Traffic light display for three major intersectionsDisplays X, Y positions and speed of up to 3 cars as they traverse each of the 3 intersections

Each component runs on separate threads

Loop through the simulation with button(s) in the GUI which provides the ability to start, pause, stop and continue the simulation.

Includes the ability to add more cars and intersections to the simulation
